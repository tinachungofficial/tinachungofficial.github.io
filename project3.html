Music App Redesign ux/ui design

Project Description

This project is a design of a music application that takes out the flaws and bad designs in existing solutions. The features of this music application include music management with playlists, categorization based on genres, songs, artists, etc. and browsing for music across the application.

Process

The first step in this redesign is to interview users of popular music applications out right now. In these interviews, we asked users about their likes and dislikes. We discovered how they use their applications and came up with user scenarios. We continued the process by doing competitive analysis across different music applications and saw commonalities and also differences. We looked at the way different music applications organized their pages and also what genres they had to expedite user's search processes. We compared the flows of different music applications and decided what were less good flows and what were better flows. The final step before wireframing was making design decisions based on the information that we have found.

My Role/Responsibilities

  • Conducted interviews to find likes and dislikes of existing solutions and discover different user scenarios
  • Competitive Analysis of Apple Music with Spotify and SoundCloud
  • Assisted in design decisions such as which features to include, what content should be on each view
